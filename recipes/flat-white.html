Flat White

Description

A Flat White is a coffee drink consisting of espresso and steamed milk. It generally has a higher proportion of espresso to milk than a usual coffee latte.

Ingredients

  • Coffee (Expresso if posible)
  • Milk (whole milk or oat milk preferably)

Steps

  1. In case of expresso make sure you are getting a 1:2 ratio of ground coffee to liquid of coffee.
  2. Pour ur coffee in a preferably rounded cup.
  3. Steam the milk with microfoam, remember you dont want a lot of foam, is not a cappuchino.
  4. Pour the milk into the coffee, you should get a 1:3 coffee to milk ratio.
