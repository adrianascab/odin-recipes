Bread and Nutella

This recipe is all about having a nice bread and a lot of nutella to put on it. There is a lot of fat on these but its really nice to have from time to time.

Ingredients

  • Nutella
  • Nice Bread

Directions

  1. Open the nutella bottle and grab a knife.
  2. Cover the knife with nutella and put in on a nice slice of bread (you can also heat the bread).
  3. Done, nice.
