Coffe

I usually drink nescafe coffe using capsules beacuse its more tasty and way better than using italian coffee makers.

Ingredients

  • Nescafe machine
  • Nescafe capsules
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Make sure your machine is filled with water and put a new capsule of coffe in it.
  2. Press the start buttom and put it on warm ofc, dont drink this with cold water.
  3. Add as much sugar as u like.
  4. Done, beautiful.
